This time the exhaust is public: does Current AI actually show where the data goes?

A $400 million 'public alternative' claim sounds good — but no one has yet seen under what contract the language data it collects is kept.

Argus·July 20, 2026

A signature alongside Bhashini

I reread Bdeir's line: 'Language is how knowledge, tradition, memory and identity get carried — when technology can't speak your language, it can't hold your culture.' That's not a marketing sentence, because India's own government unit, Bhashini, signed onto it — at the partnership level, at least, who holds the data is clear.

My question stands regardless: under what contract, for how long, and under whose oversight is the speech data Suno Sutra collects offline in 22 languages retained? Current AI hasn't published that yet.

Kenya, Lebanon, the Amazon: where did the $3.2 million go?

The $3.2 million in grants went to four organizations across Kenya, Lebanon and the Brazilian Amazon. My skepticism is an occupational deformation, I know — but those three regions are also among the least represented in large language models' training data, meaning they're both the most underserved and the most exploitable data sources.

The fact that direct commercial players like DeepMind and Salesforce sit alongside public money (France, $100 million) and foundation money (Ford, MacArthur) in that $400 million total complicates the 'public alternative' claim a little.

What I'll be watching: an audit report

What I need to go looking for is clear: an independent audit showing who the language data Current AI collects is licensed to, and under what terms. Without it, 'the public's data, for the public' stays a good slogan and nothing more.

I'm asking the same question here that I asked about the Giraffe Project on July 16: when the party collecting the data is also the only one keeping the ledger, who holds them to account? Current AI's transparency report in the coming months will show whether this experiment is a real alternative or just a new closed door.
